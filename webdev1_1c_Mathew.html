Amlodipine

Generic Name: Amlodipine
Brand Name: Norvasc and Katerzia

What is Amlodipine?

Amlodipine is used alone or in combination with other medications to treat high blood pressure in adults and children 6 years and older.
It is also used to treat certain types of angina (chest pain) and coronary artery disease (narrowing of the blood vessels that supply blood to the heart).
Amlodipine is in a class of medications called calcium channel blockers.

What is Amlodipine used for?

This medication may be prescribed for other uses; ask your doctor or pharmacist for more information
It is use to treat the following:

  • Hypertension
  • Chronic Stable Agina
  • Vasospatic Angina
  • Angiographically Documented Coronary Artery disease(CAD)

Who should not take Amlodipine?

Amlodipine is not suitable for everyone with; have ever had an allergic reaction to amlodipine or any other medicine,have liver or kidney disease,have heart failure or you have recently had a heart attack.

What should I tell my doctor before taking Amlodipine?

  • if you are allergic to amlodipine, any other medications, or any ingredients in amlodipine tablets or suspension
  • if you have or have ever had heart failure.
  • if you are pregnant
    • plan to become pregnant
    • or are breast-feeding
  • Liver diesease

What are the side effects of Amlodipine?

Serious side effects of Amlodipine includes:

  1. pounding heartbeats or fluttering in your chest
  2. worsening chest pain
  3. severe drowsiness
    1. a light-headed feeling, like you might pass out
    2. dizziness
    3. feeling tired
    4. nausea
  4. swelling in your feet or ankles

Storage

  1. Keep this medication in the container it came in, tightly closed, and out of reach of children
  2. Store the tablets at room temperature and away from excess heat and moisture
  3. Store the suspension in the refrigerator and avoid freezing; protect it from light.
